Exhibit A

This is a snippet from our upcoming report on Understanding the Spiritual Self and I could say that these are all just a meme compilation.

Actually, due to the online setting, people have been experiencing a loss in attention span and it is quite difficult for some or even many of us to focus everything on the reports, lectures and etc. This is probably due to the environmental setting of students, or even the eyestrain that causes students to go away from the screen from time to time. So I came up with the solution of using memes because in our generation, this have become a part of our internet culture and quite appealing to us and gets our attention really well because they are partly funny due to their relatable images or even outrightly nonsensical.
