The Biological Self

The biological self is the physical aspect of our existence. It is the tangible portion of our existence and it depends on our structure, heredity, health which define physical characteristics as well as our appetites which include our nutritional and sexual needs.

Physical Self

Our life is a road and our bodies are cars that takes us somewhere and somehow, just like cars we have needs just like gas, and maintenance. This is comparable to our ingestion of food, and drinnks which energize us in every aspect of our life.

For me, what I am driving I guess is a minivan with full of electronics in it. Its interior actually resembles a spy fiction's stakeout van or something as I think I my life has really been intertwined with technology. Everyday, I am exposed to technology and I am really into making and fixing these stuff as well.

Sexual Self

According to Freud, life is built around tension and pleasure and these are caused by the build-up and release, respectively. However, I digress from his argument as I believe that we, as humans, are capable of free will and we can do what we want regardless of sexual interest. That not all in life is determined by sex and sex does not determine our life.

We have the power to choose what we want to be unlike other animals which are all trying to survive. Unlike them, we make a living, and have a different purpose other than sex.

Anyhow, our primal instinct to procreate and continue the lifeline of our species can be powerful. However, we are realizing that there exists people that do not adhere to this instinct and find connections, physically or in other aspects.
