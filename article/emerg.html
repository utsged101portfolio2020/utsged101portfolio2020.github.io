The Emergent Self

What we are now, and what we perceive us to be in the future will matter in the future.

Political Self

"Man is a political animal." — Aristotle

Politics is affects everybody. Whether one likes it or not, humans are political by nature and are going to be affected by it. I myself, even not in any public office, am part of the politics in the Philippines as a registered voter and a soi‐disant advocate/activist also.

It is because what we let the politicians do now, is going to be our present and future and we have a say in that. And it is our responsibility to take care of our present and future because those are for us too.

Socio-Digital Self

It cannot be avoided that when being part of the cyberspace, internet will become a part of that someone too (especially now online classes really affected me and ruined my eyesight). Anyways, regarding the internet, what's on the internet are there to remain forever as there is nothing absolutely secure from being taken away in the digital world. Future code-breaking methods may be able to bypass the tightest web security ever with the help of quantum computing, thus it is vital for oneself to watch out for one's socio-digital self even in private online communities.

This is because what's in real life can affect one's internet life and the other way around. Thus it is best for one to be cautious and responsible in both fronts and because the future will be different from what it is now and one must anticipate the inevitability that digital technology, which is our strength now, may be our weakness someday when it is used maliciously.

Future Self

I think planning for the future (as an individual) is a dumb idea given the lessons COVID‐19 gave to us — not everything goes into plan because someone, somehow will eat a sick bat and ruin it for everyone. Instead, as an alternative, setting goals is the best option because somehow, even if a person screws up everything youv&e planned for, atleast you still have a goal in mind to reach and motivate you to be flexible and adaptable.
