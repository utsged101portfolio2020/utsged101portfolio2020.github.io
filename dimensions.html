6 DIMENSIONS OF THE SELF

The Biological Self

According to physical sciences, the human self is comprised of the physical and even sexual aspect.

The Social Self

Societal factor such as cultural, socio-digital, material, and economic aspects of life are what comprises our humanity.

The Mental Self

"Mind over matter." A powerful portion of the self which is facilitated by the rational thinking brain is the mental self comprised of cognition, intelligence, memory, and learning.

The Emotional Self

Humans are emotional beings able to feel and empathize with others with the help of their emotional intelligence and regulation.

The Spiritual Self

A human has a distinct soul and has a genuine self and connection among nature, other humans, and even a higher being.

The Emergent Self

The human self has a future that lies on their political, socio-digital, and forward thinking.
